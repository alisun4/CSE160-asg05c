It's late afternoon, the sun casting a golden glow through the cafe windows as you wrap up a fruitful study session with friends. There's no rush, as your deadlines are far away.

You're savoring delicious pastries bursting with fruit jam and sipping a rich vanilla latte. The cafe cat has even graced you with her presence, curling up on your table and taking a nap in the warm golden rays.

Life is good.
